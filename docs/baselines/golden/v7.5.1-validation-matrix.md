## Transcript and call evidence

The historical reproduction call `1784679445.962` demonstrated complete audio
with clipped assistant prefixes in Call History. The v7.5.1 regression suite
replays caller-final events before, during, and after assistant deltas; modern
and legacy transcript event variants; consecutive responses; multiple response
items; and cancellation for both OpenAI Realtime and Grok.

No media transport, VAD, resampler, Audio Profile, encoding, or provider-default
path changed in this hotfix. The accepted v7.5.0 audio evidence therefore remains
the audio baseline; v7.5.1 makes no new audio-quality support claim.

## Rollback boundary

The updater retains its normal pre-update backup and service rollback image.
If a replacement fails or does not become healthy, the Admin UI reports whether
the prior service stayed available or was restored. Existing audio-profile
assignments and historical Call History rows are not rewritten.
